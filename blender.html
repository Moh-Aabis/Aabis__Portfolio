Blender Projects

Lighthouse

This 3D model, crafted in Blender, showcases a lighthouse surrounded by rocky cliffs and reflective water, blending intricate modeling, texturing, and lighting skills. Using Blender's powerful tools, I sculpted the terrain with realistic detail, designed a detailed lighthouse structure, and employed shader techniques for water reflections. The lighting setup enhances the atmospheric mood, with the lighthouse emitting a soft yellow glow against the dark background. This scene demonstrates my proficiency in creating immersive environments in Blender.

2100 Train Station

This 3D scene, designed in Blender, depicts a futuristic transportation hub with a sleek platform and multiple transparent, blue-lit pods resembling modern train carriages. The setting is populated with realistic, human-like figures moving toward the platform. Rows of stylized street lamps illuminate the scene, enhancing the modern yet welcoming atmosphere. With detailed textures on the floor, benches, and pods, this rendering showcases advanced modeling, crowd simulation, and lighting techniques, capturing a sophisticated, urban transit environment.
